The Best Movies According to Jérémie

My top 4 movies of all-time

Bleach

This is one my favorite anime-movie adaption although having not yet watched the anime. All throughout the movie we are given hints on how dangerour the main caracter would be if he is not controlled. the ending is all contrary though.

The Tomorrow War

Very poignant and heart-breaking Sci-fi and action packed movie. The ending is tragic as it ends on the loss of the actor's beloved wife in exchange for a win for humanity.

Pacific Rim

A very futuristic and out of this world movie. The movie actually has everything to please its audience with high action packed fight scenes between humans and Kaijus(some sorts of aliens).

Purple Hearts

Excellent start to the film which gives no signs of a possible love story at all but near the end the movie becomes a near drama emotional and climatic ending.
